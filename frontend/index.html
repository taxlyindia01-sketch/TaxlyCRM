Everything You Need to Manage Finances

A complete invoicing toolkit built for Indian businesses with GST compliance at the core.

GST Tax Invoices
Fully compliant Tax Invoices with automatic CGST/SGST or IGST. HSN/SAC codes, pure agent billing and Bill-to/Ship-to addresses supported.
Proforma Invoices
Create professional quotations and convert any estimate to a Tax Invoice in one click — items and totals carry over automatically.
Client Management
Full CRM with GSTIN, PAN, outstanding balance tracking. Bulk import via Excel template, per-client ledger and overdue alerts.
Payment Tracking
Record full or partial payments via UPI, NEFT, RTGS, cheque or cash. Outstanding balances update automatically per client.
GSTR Reports
Export GSTR-1 (B2B & B2C), Sale Register, Payment Register, Outstanding Register, TDS Receivable and Complete Business Report.
Multi-Currency
Full support for INR, USD, EUR and GBP with currency-wise dashboard breakdown for exporters tracking foreign receivables.
